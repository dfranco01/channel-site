What This Brand is About

With such a vast amount of content out there in the information age, it is easy to become overwhelmed. What separates myself from other content creators is my ability to separate personal emotions from mature conversations, as well as my 'scientific' approach to dating. Unfortunately, most dating content is negative and resentful towards the other side (i.e. men hating on women and vice-versa), this channel is unbiased and focused solely on what yields desirable results, irrespective of how the message may be percieved by societal norms.

Research -> plan -> execute -> reflect -> repeat. This is all about using the scientific method to yield the results we desire in the dating scene. This is not a 'pickup' brand. This goes much deeper. In addition to the scientific approach, I believe in applying business principles in my strategies, as, like it or not, dating and business share many similarities: positioning, negotiation, value creation, and competition. My brand is all about getting results, as well as positioning oneself above the competition and negotiating well. I am excited to have you here. Welcome aboard.
